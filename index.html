Background

Pascal Neis previously ran a studies in 2010, 2011, and 2016 to identify areas underrepresented (unmapped) in OSM

The most recent study is from January 2020 and has identified hundreds of thousands places in OSM

Methodology

Use every place node of the OSM dataset which has a village-tag (place=village).

Search in a radius of ca. 700 m for a street with one of the following highway-values: residential, unclassified or service.

If no street can be found, mark the place as “unmapped”!

What did Rory do?

Created a script to join the data with Natural Earth Administrative boundaries using mapshaper

Filtered the "unmapped" areas by country

Created example mapRoulette Projects
